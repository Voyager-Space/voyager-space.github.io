About Me

Degree: Bachelor of Engineering (EEE)

Specialisation: Electrical & Electronics Engineering

City: Bengaluru, India

Email: iameshanofficial@gmail.com

Throughout my academic journey, I’ve built strong experience across embedded electronics, robotics, and physics-based simulations. I’m currently pursuing my B.E. in Electrical & Electronics Engineering at DSCE and actively contributing to engineering projects that combine electronics, software, and applied physics.

I work with the Avionics Division at Cygnus. I’ve been developing a detailed water-rocket and model-rocket flight simulation framework, focusing on accurate physics modelling, real-time visualization, and multi-parameter optimization.

My technical interests revolve around microcontrollers, embedded systems, ROS, robotics kinematics, control theory, simulation tools, and high-performance Python/C/C++ workflows. I also explore robot design, with active work on a hexapod robotics project, CAD modelling, and embedded control pipelines.

I enjoy working at the intersection of hardware and software — whether it's designing circuits, building simulations, or programming robotics systems. I regularly experiment with avionics sensors, electronics setups, and physics-based systems, while also improving my skills in Linux, ROS, and modern engineering toolchains.

Outside of engineering, I enjoy drawing anime, watching science and space content, exploring physics ideas, and creating technical content that simplifies complex topics.

"If it involves rockets, robots, electronics, or a good engineering challenge — that’s usually where I am."
